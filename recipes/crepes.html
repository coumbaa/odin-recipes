Crepes

Description

This simple but delicious crepe recipe can be made in minutes from ingredients that everyone has on hand. Crepes are very thin pancakes. They can be served with a wide variety of sweet and savory fillings and toppings. You'll find a step-by-step recipe below!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, water, and salt together in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add flour and butter and whisk vigorously until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high head. Pour or scoop the batter onto the pan, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe.
  4. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  5. Cook until the top of the crepe is no longer wet and the bottom has turned light brown, 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. Run a spatula around the edge of the skillet to loosen the crepe; flip and cook until the other side has turned light brown, about 1 minute more. Serve hot.
